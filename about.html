ABOUT ME

Raised in Central California, and now servicing the beautiful Big Island of Hawaii; Courtney is a licensed professional of 8 years, with a strong passion and extensive background in Bridal and Special Event hair. Continuously educating herself with latest trends, she possesses the knowledge technique and precision, you would expect from your hairstylist.

Courtney’s passion began when she was just a little girl practicing on her dolls, to later becoming her friends “go-to-gal” in her school years. She knew that her career was destined to involve: hairspray, a comb, and bobby pins! Braids, waves, up, down, polished, or textured, Courtney has mastered it all! Her drive stems from her client’s reaction when they look in the mirror, seeing them feel so special and beautiful. Every satisfied client gets her even more excited for the next!

From the very first email to the day of service, it is her mission to ensure that each client receive a personally tailored experience, remain stress free, and create lifetime memories to cherish. Whether you’ve got a wedding, photo shoot, school dance, or just a girls night out, rest assured Courtney will be there to deliver outstanding results! So, what are you waiting for?
